## Context
Manual access reviews, remediation tracking, and reporting created recurring effort and audit pressure.

## My Role
Security analyst / architect driving automation and reporting improvements

## What I Automated / Improved
- Streamlined access certification execution and evidence readiness
- Improved SoD remediation tracking and visibility using reporting and dashboards
- Reduced repeated manual steps through automation-first mindset

## Impact
- **200+ hours/year** saved through automation (equivalent to ~5 FTE weeks)
- Faster audit response and improved review traceability
- Better transparency into baseline risk exposure and remediation progress
