## Context
Global MDG rollout required a scalable security model for Business Partner and Material governance, supporting a large user base and strict compliance needs.

## My Role
Onshore coordinator / solution architect for MDG security and governance

## What I Delivered
- Designed MDG security framework across domains (Business Partner, Material)
- Built and governed role structures (300+ MDG-specific roles)
- Defined SoD rules for master data creation/changes to prevent unauthorized modifications
- Supported Fiori-based governance for improved usability and administration

## Scale
Supported global governance processes and enterprise user populations.

## Impact
- Improved governance maturity and reduced access ambiguity
- Stronger control coverage for master data change processes
- Reduced manual effort through scalable role design principles
